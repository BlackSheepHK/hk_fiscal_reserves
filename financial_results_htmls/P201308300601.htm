Financial results for the four months ended July 31, 2013
*********************************************************

     The Government announced today (August 30) its financial results for the four months ended July 31, 2013.

     Expenditure for the period April to July 2013 amounted to HK$138.4 billion and revenue HK$111.3 billion, resulting in a deficit of HK$27.1 billion.

     A government spokesperson said that the deficit for the period was mainly because some major types of revenue including salaries and profits taxes were mostly received towards the end of a financial year.

     The fiscal reserves stood at HK$706.8 billion as at July 31, 2013.
