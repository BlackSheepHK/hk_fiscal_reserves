Financial results for the five months ended August 31, 2011
***********************************************************

     The Government announced today (September 30) its financial results for the five months ended August 31, 2011.

     Expenditure for the period April to August 2011 amounted to $129.8 billion and revenue $128.1 billion, resulting in a deficit of $1.7 billion.

     A Government spokesman said that the deficit for the period was mainly because some major types of revenue including salaries and profits taxes were mostly received toward the end of a financial year.

     The fiscal reserves stood at $593.7 billion as at August 31, 2011.
