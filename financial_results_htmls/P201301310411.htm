Financial results for the nine months ended December 31, 2012
*********************************************************

     The Government announced today (January 31) its financial results for the nine months ended December 31, 2012.

     There was a surplus of HK$51.7 billion in the month of December, thereby bringing a net surplus of HK$40 billion up to end of December, 2012. Expenditure for the nine-month period amounted to HK$281.4 billion and revenue HK$321.4 billion.

     A government spokesperson said that the surplus in December was mainly due to the receipt of HK$37.7 billion investment income on fiscal reserves. The revised estimates for the current financial year will be published along with the 2013-14 Budget on February 27, 2013.

     The fiscal reserves stood at HK$709.1 billion as at December 31, 2012.
