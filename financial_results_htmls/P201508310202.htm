Financial results for the four months ended July 31, 2015
*********************************************************

     The Government announced today (August 31) its financial results for the four months ended July 31, 2015.

     Expenditure for the period April to July 2015 amounted to HK$141.1 billion and revenue HK$109.4 billion, resulting in a cumulative year-to-date deficit of HK$31.7 billion.

     A government spokesperson said that the cumulative year-to-date deficit for the period was mainly because some major types of revenue including salaries and profits taxes were mostly received towards the end of a financial year.

     The fiscal reserves stood at HK$796.9 billion as at July 31, 2015.
