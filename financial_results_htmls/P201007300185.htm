Financial results for the three months ended June 30, 2010
**********************************************************

     The Government announced today (July 30) its financial results for the three months ended June 30, 2010.

     Expenditure for the period April to June 2010 amounted to $71.1 billion and revenue $60.8 billion, resulting in a deficit of $10.3 billion.

     A government spokesman said that the deficit for the period was mainly because some major types of revenue including salaries and profits taxes were mostly received towards the end of a financial year.

     The fiscal reserves stood at $509.9 billion as at June 30, 2010.
