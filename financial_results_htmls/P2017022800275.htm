Financial results for the 10 months ended January 31, 2017
**********************************************************
     The Government announced today (February 28) its financial results for the 10 months ended January 31, 2017.
 
     There was a surplus of HK$46.5 billion in the month of January, thereby bringing a cumulative year-to-date surplus of HK$111.9 billion. Expenditure for the 10-month period amounted to HK$377.2 billion and revenue HK$489.1 billion.
 
     The fiscal reserves stood at HK$954.8 billion as at January 31, 2017.
 
     A government spokesperson said that the surplus in January was mainly due to the collection of salaries tax and profits tax. Taking into account the projected revenue and expenditure towards the year-end, the surplus for the 2016-17 financial year is expected to decrease and the fiscal reserves are estimated to be HK$935.7 billion as at the end of March 2017.
