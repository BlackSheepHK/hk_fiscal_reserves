Financial results for the three months ended June 30, 2004

*********************************************************

The Government announced today (July 31) its financial results for the three months ended June 30, 2004.

Expenditure for the period April to June 2004, amounted to $58.8 billion and revenue to $41.8 billion, resulting in a deficit of $17 billion.

A government spokesman said that a deficit in the initial months of a financial year was not unusual as many types of revenue including taxes were mostly received towards the end of the year.

The fiscal reserves stood at $258.3 billion at June 30, 2004.

More detailed figures are shown in Tables 1 and 2.
