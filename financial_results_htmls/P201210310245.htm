Financial results for the six months ended September 30, 2012
*********************************************************

     The Government announced today (October 31) its financial results for the six months ended September 30, 2012.

     Expenditure for the period April to September 2012 amounted to HK$190.6 billion and revenue HK$132.6 billion, resulting in a deficit of HK$58 billion.

     A government spokesperson said that the deficit for the period was mainly because some major types of revenue including salaries and profits taxes were mostly received towards the end of a financial year.

     The fiscal reserves stood at HK$611.1 billion as at September 30, 2012.
