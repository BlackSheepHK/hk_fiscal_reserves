Financial results for the two months ended May 31, 2003

*******************************************************

The Government announced today (June 30) its financial results for the two months ended May 31, 2003.

Expenditure for the period April to May 2003 amounted to $40.5 billion and revenue to $23.8 billion, resulting in a deficit of $16.7 billion.

A Government spokesman said that a deficit in the initial months of a financial year was not unusual as many major types of revenue including taxes were mostly received towards the end of a financial year.

The fiscal reserves stood at $298.8 billion at May 31, 2003.
