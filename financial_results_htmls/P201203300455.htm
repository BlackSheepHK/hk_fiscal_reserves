Financial results for the 11 months ended February 29, 2012
***********************************************************

     The Government announced today (March 30) its financial results for the 11 months ended February 29, 2012.

     There was a deficit of HK$18 billion in the month of February, thereby bringing the net surplus down to HK$84.5 billion up to end of February.  Expenditure for the 11-month period amounted to HK$328.7 billion and revenue HK$413.2 billion.

     A Government spokesperson said that, based on past experience, expenditure in March will exceed revenue, resulting in a deficit for the month and further reducing the cumulative surplus.

     The fiscal reserves stood at HK$679.9 billion as at February 29, 2012.
