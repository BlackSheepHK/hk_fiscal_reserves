Financial results for the month ended April 30, 2010
****************************************************

     The Government announced today (May 31) its financial results for April 2010, the first month of the current financial year.

     Expenditure for the first month amounted to $22.6 billion and revenue $22.4 billion, resulting in a deficit of $0.2 billion.

     A government spokesman said that the deficit for the period was mainly because some major types of revenue including salaries and profits taxes were mostly received towards the end of a financial year.

     The fiscal reserves stood at $520.1 billion as at April 30, 2010.
