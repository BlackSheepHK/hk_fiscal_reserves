Financial results for eleven months ended February 28, 2003

***********************************************************

The Government announced today (March 31) its financial results for the eleven months ended February 28, 2003.

Expenditure for the period April 2002 to February 2003 amounted to $216.9 billion and revenue to $149.2 billion, resulting in a deficit of $67.7 billion.

The fiscal reserves stood at $304.8 billion at February 28, 2003.

More detailed figures are shown in Tables 1 and 2.
