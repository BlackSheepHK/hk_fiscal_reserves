Financial results for the month ended April 30, 2013
****************************************************

     The Government announced today (May 31) its financial results for April 2013, the first month of the current financial year.

     Expenditure for the first month amounted to HK$30.2 billion and revenue HK$43.6 billion, resulting in a surplus of HK$13.4 billion.

     A government spokesperson said that the surplus in April was mainly due to the receipt of land premium.

     The fiscal reserves stood at HK$747.3 billion as at April 30, 2013.
