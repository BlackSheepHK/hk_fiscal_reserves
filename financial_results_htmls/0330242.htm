Financial results for the eleven months ended February 28, 2001

*******************************************************

The Government announced today (March 31) its financial results for the eleven months ended February 28, 2001.

Expenditure for the period April 2000 to February 2001 amounted to $200.4 billion and revenue to $191.6 billion, resulting in a deficit of $8.8 billion.

The fiscal reserves stood at $435.5 billion at February 28, 2001.

More detailed figures are shown in Tables 1 and 2.
