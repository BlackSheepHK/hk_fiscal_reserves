Financial results for the three months ended June 30, 2013
**********************************************************

     The Government announced today (July 31) its financial results for the three months ended June 30, 2013.

     Expenditure for the period April to June 2013 amounted to HK$88.6 billion and revenue HK$77.5 billion, resulting in a deficit of HK$11.1 billion.

     A government spokesperson said that the deficit for the period was mainly because some major types of revenue including salaries and profits taxes were mostly received towards the end of a financial year.

     The fiscal reserves stood at HK$722.8 billion as at June 30, 2013.
